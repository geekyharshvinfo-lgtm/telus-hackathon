Frequently Asked Questions

Office Culture & Environment

What is TELUS's work culture like?

TELUS fosters a collaborative, inclusive, and innovation-driven culture. We believe in giving back to our communities, supporting team member growth, and maintaining a healthy work-life balance. Our core values include putting customers first, being passionate about growth, and having the courage to innovate.

What are the office hours and dress code?

Our standard office hours are 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM, Monday to Friday, with flexible arrangements available. We maintain a business casual dress code, with casual Fridays. Remote work options are available depending on your role and team requirements.

What employee benefits does TELUS offer?

TELUS offers comprehensive benefits including health and dental coverage, retirement savings plans, professional development opportunities, wellness programs, employee discounts, and volunteer time off to support community involvement.

New Joiner Information

What should I expect on my first day?

On your first day, you'll receive your equipment, complete onboarding paperwork, meet your team, and attend orientation sessions. Your manager will provide a detailed schedule and introduce you to key team members and resources.

How do I access company systems and applications?

Your IT credentials will be provided during onboarding. You'll receive access to email, internal systems, and necessary applications based on your role. Contact IT Support if you experience any access issues.

Where can I find company policies and procedures?

All company policies, procedures, and employee handbook are available on the internal portal. You can also access them through the Documents section of this platform or contact HR for specific policy questions.

How do I request time off or report sick leave?

Time off requests should be submitted through the HR portal or discussed with your manager. For sick leave, notify your manager as soon as possible and follow the company's sick leave policy outlined in the employee handbook.

General Questions

How do I update my personal information?

Personal information updates can be made through the HR portal or by contacting HR directly. This includes address changes, emergency contacts, banking information, and other personal details.

Who do I contact for parking and building access?

For parking permits, building access cards, and facility-related questions, contact Facilities Support. They handle all building services, security access, and office maintenance requests.

How can I provide feedback or suggestions?

We welcome feedback and suggestions! You can share them with your manager, through employee surveys, the suggestion box, or by contacting HR. Your input helps us improve our workplace and processes.
